Intelligent Syllabus Parsing

Upload any syllabus or calendar. Our AI uses a custom-trained Named Entity Recognition (NER) model to find every `ASSESSMENT`, `TOPIC`, and `DEADLINE`. It then uses Relation Extraction to link them, building a complete knowledge graph of your course instantly.

Product Roadmap

We are building in focused phases to deliver a robust and polished product.

Phase 1: Core Engine (MVP)

Launch with core features: English-only syllabus parsing, calendar generation, and manual grade tracking. Focus on data collection to refine the AI model.

Phase 2: Learning & Language

Integrate the fine-tuned IndicBERT model for regional language support. Roll out the NPTEL/YouTube resource recommendation engine and WhatsApp reminders.

Phase 3: Collaboration & B2B

Introduce peer study group features. Launch the institutional portal with admin dashboards and analytics, initiating pilot programs with partner colleges.

Phase 4: Personalization at Scale

Implement truly adaptive study plans that adjust based on user performance and feedback. Introduce advanced goal-setting and competitive exam prep modules.
